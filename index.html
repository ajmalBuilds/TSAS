KEY ISSUES

PROLONGED TENURE OF VICE CHANCELLOR

The prolonged tenure of the Vice Chancellor, in contrast to the regular rotation observed in other state universities.

RECRUITMENT OF PERMANENT FACULTY

Recruitment processes for both teaching (assistant professors) and non-teaching staff should be transitioned to permanent appointments.

INADEQUATE AVAILABILITY OF MEDICINES

Inadequate availability of essential medicines and limited access to medical professionals in our campus hospital pose grave risks to student health.

INSTALLATION OF NEW INTERNET INFRASTRUCTURE

The installation of new Internet infrastructure in both hostel and academic blocks to ensure uninterrupted internet access for students.

SCARCITY OF SPORTS EQUIPMENT

Scarcity of sports equipment deprives students of recreational and fitness opportunities crucial for their overall well-being.

DELAYS IN FEES REIMBURSEMENT

Delays in fees reimbursement place undue financial strain on students, compounded by arbitrary demands for payment when requesting academic certificates.

COMPREHENSIVE BOOKS IN LIBRARY

Central library facilities must include a comprehensive collection of books relevant to competitive exams, essential for students aspiring to pursue higher education.

CREATION OF NCC CAMP

The creation of an NCC (National Cadet Corps) camp within the university. This initiative will foster discipline, leadership, and a sense of patriotism among students.
